A tool that automatically scans books displayed in Kindle for PC and generates PDFs optimized for E-Ink devices like the Remarkable2.

## üöÄ How to Use

![UI Image](https://github.com/RyuheiRay/Jisui-kindle2pdf/blob/master/src/assets/screenshot.png)

1. **Download and Setup**
   - Download the latest `Jisui-kindle2pdf.zip` from the [Releases](https://github.com/RyuheiRay/Jisui-kindle2pdf/releases) page and extract it.

2. **Launch the App**
   - Double-click `Jisui-kindle2pdf.exe` in the extracted folder to start.
   - **Note for Windows Users**: You may see a "Windows protected your PC" (SmartScreen) warning. This is a standard warning for unsigned indie software. Click **"More info"** and then **"Run anyway"** to proceed.

3. **Prepare Kindle (Left Button)**
   - Click the **"1. Open Kindle"** button to launch the Kindle app.
   - Open the book you wish to convert.

4. **„ÄêImportant„ÄëManual Layout Adjustment**
   - **Adjust Appearance**: To ensure the best reading experience on your device, you **must manually adjust the font size, font type, column width, and margins** via the "Aa" menu in the Kindle app.
   - **Reason**: This tool captures the screen exactly as it appears. These adjustments directly determine the readability of the final PDF.
   - **Display Mode**: Ensure the app is set to "Single Page View".

5. **Start Conversion (Middle Button)**
   - Click the **Arrow Button (2. Start!)** to begin.
   - **[Note]**: The tool will automatically navigate to the first page and enter full-screen mode. Please do not touch your mouse or keyboard until the capture process is complete.

6. **Check the PDF (Right Button)**
   - Once finished, click the **"3. Open Folder"** button. Your completed PDF will be in the `result` folder.
